Policy: MRI Lumbar Spine without Contrast\n\nCriteria for Approval:\n1. Patient has experienced low back pain for at least 6 weeks.\n2. Conservative therapy (e.g., physical therapy, NSAIDs) has been attempted and failed.\n3. Red flag symptoms (e.g., progressive neurological deficit, suspected infection/malignancy) are present OR conservative therapy failed.\n4. Imaging study requested: MRI Lumbar Spine without contrast.\n\nRequired Information:\n- Duration of symptoms (in weeks/months).\n- Details of conservative therapies tried (type, duration, outcome).\n- Presence or absence of red flag symptoms (specify if present).\n- Exact imaging study being requested.
